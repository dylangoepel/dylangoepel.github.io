Derivation of the Moivre Binet formula

\[\begin{aligned} F_0 &:= 0 \\ F_1 &:= 1 \\ F_{n + 2} &:= F_n + F_{n + 1} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} F := \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} \begin{pmatrix} F_{n + m} \\ F_{n + m + 1} \end{pmatrix} = F^n \cdot \begin{pmatrix} F_n \\ F_{n + 1} \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} \mathcal{P} &:= \begin{pmatrix} -\phi & -\psi \\ 1 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \\ \mathcal{P}^{-1} &= \begin{pmatrix} -\sqrt{5}/5 & (5 - \sqrt{5})/10 \\ \sqrt{5}/5 & (5 + \sqrt{5})/10 \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} \mathcal{P}^{-1} \cdot F \cdot \mathcal{P} = \begin{pmatrix} \psi & 0 \\ 0 & \phi \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} F^n &= \mathcal{P} \cdot \begin{pmatrix} \psi^n & 0 \\ 0 & \phi^n \end{pmatrix} \cdot \mathcal{P}^{-1} \\\ &= \begin{pmatrix} \phi^n \frac{5 - \sqrt{5}}{10} + \psi^n \frac{5 + \sqrt{5}}{10} & \frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}(\phi^n - \psi^n) \\ \frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}(\phi^n - \psi^n) & \psi^n \frac{5 - \sqrt{5}}{10} + \phi^n \frac{5 + \sqrt{5}}{10} \end{pmatrix} \end{aligned}\] \[\begin{aligned} F_n &= \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} \cdot F^n \cdot \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \\ &= \frac{\phi^n - \psi^n}{\sqrt{5}} \end{aligned}\]
